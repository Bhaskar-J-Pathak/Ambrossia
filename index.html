About

Established 2016

Ambrossia Dessert Bar was founded in 2014 by Arthur Gray who embarked on his journey into the culinary world at the age of just 14. With the unwavering support of his family, Arthur transformed his passion for desserts into a global phenomenon.

A World of Firsts

Ambrossia stands proudly as the only awarded dessert bar in the world. From its humble beginnings, it has continuously pushed the boundaries of creativity, innovation, and excellence, crafting desserts that delight the senses and redefine indulgence.

The Ambrossia Experience

At Ambrossia, every dessert tells a story. With an emphasis on impeccable craftsmanship and the finest ingredients, the bar delivers a multi-sensory experience that keeps dessert lovers coming back for more. Whether it's a signature creation or a seasonal special, every bite is a masterpiece.
